Payment Processing Information

Stripe

Stripe is a payment service that takes care of credit card payments, and makes deposits directly to a bank account of your choosing. It comes with a easy to understand view of your payment history, and gives you direct control over refunds. The buyer's credit card information goes straight to Stripe over a secure connection without passing through our site, so your customers will know that their payments are safe.

The fees for using Stripe are currently 2.9% of the sale plus 30 cents, which is taken from the sale price of your items. Our fees are 3.5% of the sale, so the total fees on each transaction are 6.4% + 30 cents.


Other payment options

We plan to offer PayPal as a payment service in the near future. We're also interested in payment methods which have lower fees, such as Dwolla and Bitcoin. If you have a preferred payment method that we do not offer, please let us know and we'll try to incorporate it as an option down the road. Thanks!
