The cottage and artisanal food movement is building fast! Several states have passed cottage food laws that allow people to sell homemade foods with certain restrictions made right in their own home kitchens. In California, this law is called the Homemade Food Act (AB 1616).

At Homemade1616 we connect cottage and artisanal food makers with people who want to buy their delectable delights. But we do much more than that! We also give money to food-related nonprofit organizations and, soon, we hope to start micro-lending to people who want to get started in the food business but need a little assistance.
